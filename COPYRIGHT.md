## Source book

This site is a study companion to **Charles Platt's *Make: Electronics — Learning by Discovery*, 3rd Edition** (Make Community, 2021). It assumes you have your own copy of the book.

The summaries on each experiment page are written from scratch in plain language; nothing from the book itself appears here. Schematics, Arduino sketches, and circuit examples were built independently from the experiments' underlying physics.

If you don't have the book yet, grab one from [Make Community](https://www.makershed.com) or O'Reilly.
