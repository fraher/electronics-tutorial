## Source book

This site is a **paraphrased companion** to:

> Platt, Charles. *Make: Electronics — Learning by Discovery*, 3rd Edition.
> Make Community, 2021.

- No prose, photos, or illustrations from the book are reproduced.
- All summaries are original writing in my own words.
- All schematics are redrawn original artwork.
- All Arduino sketches are original code.
- All circuit example payloads (`.cir`, `diagram.json`) are constructed
  independently from the experiment's physics, not copied.

The book itself is **not** included in this repository (`docs/*.pdf` is
gitignored). If you want to follow along chapter-by-chapter, you should buy
your own copy.

## Vendored third-party code

- **CircuitJS** (Paul Falstad et al., GPL v2) lives at `public/circuitjs/` and
  is **gitignored**. It's fetched at author time via `scripts/vendor-circuitjs.sh`.
  See its `LICENSE.txt` + `NOTICE.txt` (regenerated by the vendor script).
- **wokwi-cli** and **arduino-cli** are external tools — they are not bundled.
- **Wokwi.com** is the documented online dependency for the Chapter 5 Arduino
  experiments. The "Run this in Wokwi" buttons open the official wokwi.com
  editor.
