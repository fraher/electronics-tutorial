# paraphrase-not-copy

## Decision
Every paragraph of prose on the site is original — no verbatim runs from the book. All schematics are redrawn original artwork (currently rendered as `schematic_description` prose; SVG deferred). No book images or photos reproduced.

## Why
- Copyright posture for the source book (Charles Platt, *Make: Electronics 3e*, No Starch / O'Reilly)
- Fair-use posture is personal local study — not deploy-safe
- Operator owns the source PDF

## How to apply
Any content addition must paraphrase (write closed-book, then verify no 8+ word verbatim run). Formulas themselves are facts and may be reproduced — only the surrounding prose is at issue. See [[../entities/Brief]] for the paraphrased-summary field.
